---

Who is this for, what problem does it solve, what would v2 look like? ==>

Built for SMB owners in Uzbekistan whose teams track cash through WhatsApp voice notes, paper notebooks, and scattered Excel. Captures every transaction by voice or text in Uzbek/Russian/English through a Telegram bot the team already lives in, and turns it into a clean dashboard the owner uses to monitor income, expenses, runway, and recurring patterns. v2 = receipt-photo OCR via Telegram, multi-user roles with approval flow for large expenses, and a "month close" review screen that takes 5 minutes a month.

---

What would you add next if you had 3 more days? ==>

Receipt-photo OCR — send a photo to the bot, Gemini vision pulls out merchant + items + total, pre-fills a transaction, attaches the photo. Then a "month close" review screen that walks the owner through every flagged anomaly and uncategorized entry with a one-click "looks right". Then a Telegram-side approval flow ("Karim wants to spend 4M on Inventory — approve?") so the owner stops being a bottleneck on day-to-day spend.
